Hi, I'm Kei.

I created this webapp to help my mom when she cooks for me.

I got my first kidney stones when I was 22. The doctor then told me to make sure I drink enough water, gave me a small list of foods to avoid such as spinach or potato chips, and sent me on my way.

Unsurprisingly I developed kidney stones again two years later. Never wanting to go through that pain again, I did extensive research and found that many foods I thought were "safe" were actually extremely high in oxalates, such as turmeric spice.

These lists that are published online are difficult to navigate and are often conflicting or based on poor research studies. Another problem is that I do not expect my mother/girlfriend/self to navigate these lists at each meal to ensure it is safe to eat. Therefore I have developed this webapp to address all of these issues, as well as provide oxalate content of foods that include the sources so they can be verified.

I hope this webapp is as useful to you as it has been to me and my family.
